Remember in art history class when they said that this piece by Rembrandt was from the Baroque period? Or maybe you remember learning the origins of your favorite genre of music? You got the sense that there was a particular artistic profile staple to that creative era. If you were really paying attention, you could even identify the artist, year and style. That's how we can think about Kingdom Genre.

We are Kingdom workers, an infantry armed with all mediums. Creating with the profile of God's Kingdom, we will edify our Christian community and evangelize the lost. Furthermore, God being the Creator means we ourselves are excruciating exquisite artwork native to Him. As we embrace this, we are expecting to witness the masterpieces God will produce through us as we also are achieving masterpiece status. Here at Kingdom Genre, we maintain core values of faith in Christ, gospel mission, fun, community, that which is eye-opening, pushes boundaries in an unconventional way!

~Chatelaine Dunord
